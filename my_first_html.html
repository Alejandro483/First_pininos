How to make a sandwich

  1. Put 2 pieces of bread (whole wheat preferably) on a plate.
  2. Spread some peanut butter on both pieces.
  3. Cut half a banana in small slices and put them on the bread.
  4. Cover the bananas with the second piece of bread (the PB side should be facing the bananas)
  5. Enjoy it!
